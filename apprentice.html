Apprentice character page

Name: Unknown

Species: Unknown

Power: Shapeshifting / Witchcraft

Info: Apprentice was taken in by Lilith when she was still a child and has stayed by her side ever since.
She eventually took them in as her apprentice and has been slowly helping them regain the power they lost. Their
real name and origin are unknown to everyone, even Lilith. While they stay in their small form most of the time,
they are able to shift into both a human one and one that resembles a large snake or Wyrm, this is thought to be
their true self. They have sworn loyalty to Lilith and plans to stay by her side for as long as they are able.

Power description: Apprentice is learning witchcraft from Lilith. They have a fair grasp of the elemental
magic , especially the shadow and air elements, and uses them regularly in battle. Along with this, they are able
to shift into three different forms, a small form that is agile and uses the least amount of energy to sustain, a
human form that uses moderate energy but is easier to cast spells in and a large Wyrm form that is incredibly
strong but uses vast amounts of energy, meaning they can only keep that form for short periods of time.
