Guardian character page

Name: Unknown

Species: Vessel

Power: Pure light

Info: Guardian was the first of the guardians to join and was the one who formed the group in order to protect others.
Her real name is only known to those who joined during the beginning. She was taken from her home universe to
become a vessel for light and has been unable to return. She has taken up the mantle of guardian after a great loss
that she swore to never let happen again.
