Lilith character page

Name: Lilith

Species: Witch

Power: Witchcraft

Info: Lilith has been learning magic since she was a child and has mastered all of the different branches.
She is constantly looking to push the barriers of magic and has even managed to grant herself eternal youth. While
she is the most powerful mage in existence, her and her apprentice use their powers to torment a local kingdom.
She has close ties to Melonie and routinely supply her Knights and Bishops with spells and potions.
